Introduction

Over the last decade, data from the City of Chicago's Data Portal has revealed startling trends in arrests by race. In this analysis, we explore these trends and discuss their broader implications.

The Numbers

Arrests among the Black community have decreased since 2020 but remain significantly higher than any other reporting demographic. Meanwhile, arrests among the Hispanic population have steadily increased since 2021.

Looking Ahead

These trends reflect systemic inequalities in policing practices. Addressing these disparities requires both accountability and equitable reform at every level of the justice system.
